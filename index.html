關於倍榮 About PME

About PME

倍榮公司成立於1990年, 起初公司著重於水泥業界旋窯冷卻系統的安裝，以及參與機電設備的規劃、廠房設計、機具代工。因對於環境保護的重視，我們致力於空氣品質改善系統的研發與安裝，工程技術及服務態度受到業主肯定。在2016年，我們將版圖拓展到了東南亞的新加坡及馬來西亞，希望可以將這份熱誠與環境保護的精神渲染到各個地區。 對倍榮而言,使命必達一直是承接工程的準則之一。我們從事物料處理系統之規劃、設計、製造、安裝、系統試車及機械維護。服務的產業包括紙廠、電廠、石化廠、水泥廠、焚化爐以及其它工業，其提供範圍由單一設備至整廠全套設備及系統自動控制工程。

Peiloong Mechanical Engineering was founded in 1990. At beginning, we focus on cement industry: erection of rotary kiln cooling system as well as participate in planning of mechanical & electrical equipment, plant design and equipment OEM. Due to the concept of environmental protection is become more important every day, we are committed to the development and installation of air quality improvement system. We have earned recognition for our skill and service by our client. Therefore, we decided to expand our company to Southern Asia: Singapore and Malaysia in 2016. To pass on the passion and the spirit of environmental protection to the whole world. Deliver the promise is our priority when we undertake the project. We are engaged in planning, design, manufacture, installation, commissioning and machinery maintenance. The service industries include paper mills, power plants, petrochemical plants, cement plants, incinerators, and other industries, ranging from a single equipment to a full plant and system automation control project.

企業理念corporate philosophy

倍榮公司的經營理念，是以『專業』、『負責』、『學習』、『回饋』的態度面對客戶、員工及社會。我們對客戶承諾並達成期許，堅持施工品質以最高標準為自我要求，培養員工對於技能上的學習，同時提升工作安全及環境保護意識、積極參與環保設備的建造，達到與客戶互利、與員工互助、對社會回饋的責任。

“Professional”, “Responsible”, “Improvement”, “Contribute” are the key words of our concept of management toward customer, employee and society. We are committed to the customer and reach expectations, adhere to the construction quality at the highest standards for self-demand. Training staff for improvement of skill, while improving work safety and environmental protection awareness. We actively participate in the construction of environmental protection equipment to achieve win-win solution toward our customer, employee and society.
